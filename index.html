Hi, I'm Milda Norkute

At the moment I'm in between websites. However, I've summarized some of the highlights below --

I'm currently a Lead UX Researcher and Designer at Thomson Reuters Labs. My CHI2021 paper Towards Explainable AI: Assessing the Usefulness and Impact of Added Explainability Features in Legal Document Summarization is a pretty good representation of what kind of work I do at the labs. Though most of it is confidential.

Before Thomson Reuters I worked as Designer and Front End Developer at CERN (2018-2019) and Nokia (2017 summer). I also currently have the pleasure to co-lead the Human Centred AI research theme as part of the Labs research program.

2016-2018, I completed a double masters in Human Computer Interaction and Design at Aalto University and KTH Royal Institute of Technology.

Before starting my career in technology, 2014-2016 I worked in media research at Newsworks, in London. I graduated with bachelors degree in Psychology from University Bath with first class honours in 2014. I also spent a year working in academic research at New Zealand Institute of Language Brain and Behaviour between 2012-2013.

You can download my CV in case I missed something. You can contact me at milda.norkute@bath.edu, or find me online on Twitter and LinkedIn.
